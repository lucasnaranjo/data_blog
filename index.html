Activity according to their first words

The black dot represents the average 'activity score' for learners who included early the specific word. A word is classified as 'early' if it is within the learner's first 6 words and the learner's total vocabulary did not exceed 8 words during their initial week of training. The other end of the line reflects the average score for learners who did not have the word introduced early. The size of the black dot correlates with the word's popularity as an early introduction.
The activity score is calculated based on the average number of presses per day and on the number of multi-button interactions.
