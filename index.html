User filtering criteria:
We excluded learners who logged more than 7 different buttons in their first week of collected data as they might started learning long before logging their data. We excluded learners who started logging their presses later than 2 months after they started their journey. The resulting dataset is composed of 307 pet learners.
The black dot represents the average score for learners who included early the specific word. A word is classified as 'early' if it is within the learner's first 4 words. The other end of the line is the average score for learners who did not have the word introduced early. The size of the black dot indicates the word's popularity as an early button.
